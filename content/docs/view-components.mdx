## Available components

<span id="view-components" className="block -mt-24 pt-24" />

- [Deposit](/docs/components/deposit): Deposit tokens into a destination with source breakdown, allowance flow, and optional embedded card view. Intent-based and customizable execute builder.
- [Fast Bridge](/docs/components/fast-bridge): Bridge assets across chains with source breakdown, fee details, progress steps, and allowance flow. Intent-based and optimized for UX.
- [Swaps](/docs/components/swaps): Swap assets with exact-in or exact-out across supported chains. Intent-based with prefill, callbacks, and progress UI.
- [Unified Balance](/docs/components/unified-balance): View unified token balances across chains with per-chain breakdown and fiat totals.
